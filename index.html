Who Am I?

My name is Sarah (SarahBeth), and I am currently a student at Bob Jones University in Greenville, SC. I am studying Criminal Justice and ultimately hope to attend law school.  I attend Summit View Baptist Church while I am here in SC. 

What Do I Like To Do?

I love to do a pretty big variety of activities. So, let's break them down into categories: sports, outdoor activities, and indoor activities. In the category of sports, I played basketball in high school and it was one of my favorite things to do, but I also LOVE to watch baseball. My favorite outdoor activities include hunting, fishing, hiking, camping, archery, and swimming. When I can't be outside, I enjoy reading mystery novels and Christian biographies, painting, playing the piano, and experimenting in the kitchen.

What About My Family?

I am the oldest of my mom's three kids. I also have two older brothers on my dad's side. I am very close to my family even though I no longer live at home. My mom is one of my closest friends, and I definitely have the BEST dad in the world! My little brother is by far my favorite hunting partner, and I have the most caring little sister EVER! I love them all SOOO much!!
